PhishingGuard Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-01-01

1. Data We Collect

PhishingGuard does not collect any personally identifiable information. The extension may analyze the URL and HTML content of the page you visit, but all processing is performed locally on your browser and no data is transmitted externally.

2. Data Storage

PhishingGuard does not store, save, or upload any user data to external servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. No data is used for advertising, analytics, or tracking.

4. Local Processing

All phishing detection logic happens inside the browser. The extension does not communicate with any external API to check visited websites.

5. Policy Updates

Changes to this Privacy Policy will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
